What is Industry ready?

Our vision is to create opportunities to build capabilities through practice and engagement. In Industry, it is the work output that matters. Work output is judged in terms of Skill, Knowledge, Communication and Execution. These can only be built together, and building one at the expense of the other is not useful.

Skills

Basic skills can be developed early, be it in coding in a particular language or framework, design, etc. This can happen only through repeated practice. Starting with simple skills building up to more complex capabilities and finally expertise. By increasing Skills, the work output can be delivered at a higher quality, and faster.

Learn more
Knowledge

Knowledge refers to depth in a given field or domain such as AI, Blockchain or industry sectors such as Finance. This can happen only through exposure to learning materials, Industry interactions, etc. By increasing Knowledge, the work output is line with requirements and even with future needs.

Learn more
Communication

Work output should be presented concisely and precisely. Good communication involves understanding both the content and audience. By increasing Communication, the features and qualities of work output can reach more people and more effectively.

Learn more
Execution

More important than talking about an idea, is to execute the idea and bring it to fruition. This involves collaboration with others, and planning the work. By increasing Execution, work output is delivered predictably and making the best use of available resources.

How does it work?

We build Industry-ready Students through practice on our Discord platform. We are supported by Mentors and guided through structured and gamified activities.

𝝁Learn

Improving Skills, Knowledge, Communication and Execution, through a scalable platform enabled by gamification. Our aim is create a new generation of pre-skilled Industry-Ready students.

Practice

At the lowest level, our activities are designed to build specific skills that are in demand from Industry. We encourage that through sustained practice over a longer period of time.

Discord Platform

Our Discord is a highly scalable communication platform where lakhs of participants can safely participate in the activities. Supported by our discord bot: aaronchettan.

Mentorship

𝝁Learn integrates learning communities, companies and partners, who provide Mentors. Students can access these Mentors through our platform, and gain valuable experience.

Gamification System

Motivates and moulds students by providing a set of activities to be completed. Gives students a method to track their own progress. Students' work is evaluated by Mentors.

Who is involved?

The 𝝁Learn Framework integrates participants such as Students, Industry, Online Communities, Learning Programs, into a holistic system.

  • Premier Industry Association
  • Top Universities of Kerala
  • Communities and Programs
Industry

Companies and organizations are a core participant through Mentors, Projects and Internship activities.

Universities

Top Kerala based Universities have joined hands to meet the needs of Industry and develop the capabilities of students.

Communities

Groups such as Tech Myrmidons, keralarescue, OWASP have volunteered to support students.

Mentors

Mentors are volunteers, from Industry, Academia, Communities, Open source projects who will guide and support students.

Enablers

Representatives of Colleges who organizes college/local level activities, and integrates faculty members into the system.

Advisory Board

Senior level individuals from Industry, Academia, R&D and Govt who are driving the vision in a personal capacity.
