Colleges

Companies spends huge amounts of money training fresh graduates. While they have a lot of knowledge, most of the graduates are missing core competencies right out of college, such as Skills, Communication ability, and Execution of projects from start to finish.

ATFG provides a structured and motivating means to guide students to become Industry Ready. Our activities give a glimpse of the business processes and required skills. This allows students to mould themselves during their educational periods.

  • Delegate one or more faculty members as Enablers
  • Encourage other faculty members to join as Mentors
  • Contact admins to induct the Enablers & Mentors
  • Work with ATFG to onboard your Students
  • Enablers and Mentors should support Students to complete Activities and tasks
  • See our pages on Mentors and Teachers for more ideas.
