Hop Step Jump

Students and startups can apply for our Hop Step Jump program. If you have a prototype requiring funding, or are looking for customers for your product or service, you can apply.

  • Apply for Hop Step Jump
  • Deliver an Elevator Pitch, which occurs every first Wednesday of the month
  • After shortlisting, you can present a demo every third Wednesday of the month
